BBQ Pineapple Flatbread

Overview

BBQ Pineapple Flatbread
Hello Fresh
35 minutes
2

Ingredients

Amount Ingredient Note
1 Red onion
5 tsp Red Wine Vinegar
4 oz Fresh Mozzarella
0.25 oz Cilantro
4 TBSP BBQ Sauce
4 oz Pineapple
1 Poblano Pebber
0.25 cup Monterey Jack Cheese Dairy
2 Flatbreads Wheat

Instructions

Step 1

  • Place a foil-lined baking sheet on top rack; preheat oven to 450F
  • Wash and dry all produce
  • Halve, peel, nad thinly slice onion
  • Drain pinapple over a small bown, reserving juice
  • Stir 1/4 of the onion, half the vinegar, and a pinch of salt into the bowl with juice

Step 2

  • heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium heat
  • Add remaining onion and cook, stirring and adding spashes of water as needed to prevent sticking
  • Stop when onion is softened (6-8 minutes)

Step 3

  • While onion cooks, halve, core, and very tinly slice poblano into strips. Toss in a small bowl with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper
  • Dice Mozzarella into 1/2 inch pieces
  • Finely chop cilantro

Step 4

  • Once onion is softened, increase head under pan to medium high
  • Add pineapple and another drizzle of oil; cook, stirring until pineapple is lightly browned (2-3 minutes)
  • Stir in remaining vinegar
  • Cook, stirring until oinon and pineapple are caramelized and jammy, (1-2 minutes)
  • Remove from heat; season with salt and pepper

Step 5

  • Brush or rub each flatbread with a drizzle of oil
  • Remove prepared baking sheet from oven, acrefully place flatbreads on it
  • Evenly spread with BBA sauce
  • Top with caramelized onion and pineapple, poblano, Montery Jack, and mozzarella
  • Return flatbreads to top rack until cheese has melted and edges are golden brown (10-12 minutes)
  • Transfer to a cutting board
  • Top with cilantro and pickled onion (drain first)
